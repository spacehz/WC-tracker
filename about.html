About Feen El Match

Feen El Match is a free, independent guide to the 2026 FIFA World Cup. We bring together the full match schedule, convert every kick-off into your local timezone automatically, and show you which TV channels and streaming services are carrying each match in your country.

Why we built it

With 48 teams and matches spread across the United States, Canada and Mexico, kick-off times land at awkward hours all over the world, and broadcast rights differ in every country. We wanted one simple, fast page that answers two questions: when is the match and where can I watch it.

How it works

Match fixtures and live scores are sourced from a football data provider and refreshed automatically. Broadcaster listings are maintained by us per country. Availability can change, so always double-check your local listings near kick-off.
